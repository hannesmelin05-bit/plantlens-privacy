Privacy Policy — Last updated: June 1, 2026

PlantLens ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Photos: When you identify a plant, your photo is sent to the Plant.id API for identification. Photos you choose to share to the Community feed are uploaded to our secure storage.
  • Account information: If you create an account, we store your email address.
  • Community posts: Plant name, optional caption, optional location, and photo for posts you share.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify plants using the Plant.id service
  • To display your posts in the Community feed
  • To save your plant collection to your account
  • To improve the app experience

3. Third-Party Services

PlantLens uses the following third-party services:

  • Plant.id — plant identification API. Photos are sent to their servers for analysis. See their privacy policy at plant.id.
  • Supabase — database and file storage. Data is stored securely. See supabase.com/privacy.

4. Camera and Photo Library

PlantLens requests access to your camera and photo library solely to enable plant identification. We do not access your photos for any other purpose. Photos are only sent to Plant.id when you actively request an identification.

5. Data Retention

Your plant collection and community posts are stored as long as your account is active. You can delete individual posts at any time from the Community screen. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

PlantLens is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
